## Introduction to Extended Reality

#### What is AR/VR/AV/MR/XR and how do the terminologies compare?

Extended Reality (XR) is a superset of multiple terminologies:

**Augmented Reality (AR)**

- **Virtual Reality (VR)**

- **Augmented Virtuality (AV)**

- **Mixed Reality**
